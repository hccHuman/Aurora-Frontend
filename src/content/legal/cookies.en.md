# Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience and ensure proper site functionality.

## 1. What cookies are
A cookie is a small file stored on your device that allows us to remember preferences.

## 2. Types of cookies we use
- **Technical**: required for the site to function.
- **Analytics**: help us understand how you browse.
- **Customization**: store language or preferences.
- **Advertising** (if applicable): show relevant ads.

## 3. Cookie management
You can allow, block or delete cookies directly from your browser settings.

## 4. Third-party cookies
Some cookies may be set by third-party services such as analytics tools or social media.

We may update this policy at any time.
